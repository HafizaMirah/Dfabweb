Safety

Before entering or using the machineries, we need to ensure that we are all aware of the safety and regulations of every lab and machines before proceeding with our project.

  1. Students are not allowed to enter and work in any laboratory or workshop without the supervision of the lecturer in charge or Technical Supporting Officer.
  2. Students must be in a proper attire to enter the lab
    • Do not wear loose-sleeved clothing and jewellery
