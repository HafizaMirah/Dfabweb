Safety

Before entering or using the machineries, we need to ensure that we are all aware of the safety and regulations of every lab and machines before proceeding with our project.

  1. Students are not allowed to enter and work in any laboratory or workshop without the supervision of the lecturer in charge or Technical Supporting Officer.
  2. Students must be in a proper attire to enter the lab
    • Do not wear loose-sleeved clothing and jewellery.
    • Do not use compressed air to clean your clothing.
    • Anyone with long hair should tie up their hair to prevent entanglement with rotating machinery
    • Be in collared shirt or T-shirt and full-length trousers.
    • Be in fully covered shoes at all time.
    • Different laboratories and workshops required the use of additional safety equipments.Always follow the instructions regarding the safety equipments that needs to be used before operating the machine.
      • No food and drinks allowed in the workshop or laboratories
      • Ensure that you are familiar with the placment of the first-aid box, safety eye-wash and /or shower, chemical spill kit within each laboratory and workshop in case of any emergencies.
      • Keep your work area clean before you leave the laboratory/workshop.
      • Approach your lecturer whenever youre in doubt
